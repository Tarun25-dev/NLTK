POS tagging:
>> POS means Parts of Speech
>> Identifying gramming role of each word.
>> Example sentence : text = "Tarun is learning Artificial Intelligence in India."
>> Computer identifies:
>> | Word         | Role        |
>> | ------------ | ----------- |
>> | Tarun        | Proper Noun |
>> | is           | Verb        |
>> | learning     | Verb        |
>> | Artificial   | Adjective   |
>> | Intelligence | Noun        |
>> | India        | Proper Noun |

Difference between Noun and Proper Noun

Noun: A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.
Ex: boy, city

Proper Noun: A proper noun is a specific name of a particular person, place, or thing. It always starts with a capital letter.
Ex: Tarun, Banglore
